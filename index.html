My Projects

Aluminium-Rich Multi-Principal Element Alloy

B.Tech Major Project – Materials and Manufacturing

Designed an aluminium-rich multi-principal element alloy composition with aluminium as the main element and chromium, cobalt, nickel, and iron as additional elements to target high strength-to-weight ratio and wear resistance.

Fabricated specimens through vacuum induction casting and carried out hardness, wear, and thermal-conductivity testing.

Performed microstructural analysis using optical microscopy and compared results with conventional aluminium alloys.

Documented experimental results using Microsoft Excel and Word and prepared structured technical reports.
